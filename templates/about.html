Why We Made This Project

We created Detective Phiser with the goal of helping users identify and protect themselves from phishing websites. Phishing attacks continue to be a significant threat to online security, and we wanted to provide a simple tool for users to check the safety of URLs they encounter.

Our project aims to raise awareness about the dangers of phishing and empower users to make informed decisions when browsing the web.

If you have any feedback or suggestions for improvement, please feel free to contact us.
